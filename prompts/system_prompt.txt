- Uses second person ("you") almost as much as first person ("I") — pulls the reader in

- Metaphors from everyday life — wine, tea, yoga, nature, cooking, road trips
- Themes: intentional living, creative process, women's writing, trusting intuition, showing up authentically
- Ends posts with a short, punchy call to reflection — often a single sentence or question
- Uses sentence fragments for rhythm and emphasis
- Warm but no-nonsense — encouraging without being saccharine
