Starting a blog can feel overwhelming, especially if you're not sure where to begin. So, let's get real - here are three practical tips for women who want to start blogging but don't know where to begin:

**Stop overthinking**

I know it's easy to spend hours thinking about your blog name, tagline, and design (and I'm guilty of this too!). But trust me, there's no need to overthink it.

Start by asking yourself a few simple questions: What do you want to talk about? Who do you want to help? What makes your voice unique?

Take 10-15 minutes to brainstorm, then use the answers as inspiration for your blog name and tagline. You can always change them later if you need to!

**Focus on creating content that serves others**

When I first started my business (and even now!), I was focused on "selling" myself - talking about how amazing I am and why you should work with me.

But let's get real - people don't care about your story. They care about what you can do for them. And they care about the benefits of working with you.

So, start by thinking about what problems you solve or what benefits you bring to others. Then, use that as inspiration for your blog content (posts, videos, podcasts, etc.).

**Start small and test your way up**

I see so many women who want to create a beautiful website and then...nothing else happens.

The thing is, it's not about starting with perfection - it's about taking action. You can start by creating a simple one-page site or even just posting on Facebook or Instagram (if that feels more comfortable for you).

Then, as you experiment with different types of content and see what works best for your audience, you'll get feedback from them (or not). And then, you can refine and adjust your strategy.
