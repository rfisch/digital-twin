As I continue on this path of discovery and exploration, I wanted to take a moment to talk about something that's very close to my heart: handwriting.

With the rise of digital everything (and I mean EVERYTHING), it can be tempting to think that there's no need for handwriting any more. But I'm here to tell you that that couldn't be further from the truth!

In a world where so much is virtual, handwriting remains one of the most intimate and personal ways we connect with our thoughts, feelings, and creative expressions. It's a way to tap into your inner artist (yes, YOU are an artist!) and allow yourself to let go of perfectionism and just write from the heart.

Whether you're writing in a journal, creating art on paper, or simply putting pen to paper for a letter or card, handwriting offers something that digital can't: a sense of presence. You get to feel the weight of the pen, see your words appear before your eyes, and connect with your thoughts in a way that's both tactile and emotional.

So how do you get started? I recommend getting some beautiful paper (I love making my own!) and just start writing whatever comes up for you. Don't worry about what others will think - this is all about YOU. You can also try some guided prompts on your own or with me in a future workshop!

If you're not sure where to begin, I'd be happy to help! We'll get into the details of how handwriting has helped me connect with my thoughts and emotions (and why I believe it can do the same for you).

And if you've been writing for years and are looking for new inspiration or motivation, I invite you to try something different. Maybe write with a new color pen, change up your paper or font style - there's so much fun to be had!

Let me know how you're feeling about handwriting these days. Do you still have time to write in a journal or create on paper?

I'd love for you to join the conversation! You can comment below, reply to this email, or share with your friends who might enjoy it.

Thanks so much for being part of my community and I look forward to hearing from you!
